Curriculum Overview

Detailed course list by grade level

Preschool Curriculum (Ages 1.5-3)

Subject Description Duration Frequency
Language Development Basic vocabulary, storytelling, phonics introduction 30 mins/day Daily
Early Math Concepts Counting, shapes, sorting, patterns 30 mins/day Daily
Creative Arts Drawing, painting, crafts, music appreciation 45 mins/day Daily
Physical Development Gross motor skills, coordination activities 30 mins/day Daily
Social Skills Sharing, cooperation, emotional awareness Integrated throughout day Daily

Kindergarten Curriculum (Ages 4-5)

Subject Description Duration Frequency
Language Arts Phonics, reading readiness, storytelling 45 mins/day Daily
Mathematics Numbers, basic operations, measurement 45 mins/day Daily
Science Exploration Nature studies, simple experiments 30 mins/day 3 times/week
Social Studies Community, culture, basic geography 30 mins/day 3 times/week
Creative Expression Art, music, drama activities 45 mins/day Daily

PP1 Curriculum (Ages 6-7)

Subject Description Duration Frequency
English Language Reading, writing, grammar, comprehension 60 mins/day Daily
Mathematics Arithmetic, problem-solving, geometry 60 mins/day Daily
Science Life science, physical science, earth science 45 mins/day 4 times/week
Social Studies History, geography, civics 45 mins/day 4 times/week
Kiswahili Language fundamentals, conversation 45 mins/day Daily

PP2 Curriculum (Ages 7-8)

Subject Description Duration Frequency
English Language Advanced reading, composition, grammar 60 mins/day Daily
Mathematics Fractions, decimals, measurements, geometry 60 mins/day Daily
Science Biology, chemistry, physics concepts 60 mins/day 4 times/week
Social Studies Kenyan history, world cultures, government 60 mins/day 4 times/week
Creative Arts Art, music, drama, and movement 60 mins/day 3 times/week
